A Great Sword

Stats:

Ability Demonic Resistance
Height 5 feet
Width 10 inches
Length 2.5 inches
Weight 2lbs
Item Class II

Origins:

Inventor Bartholomew
Aliases A King's Sword
Purpose Combat

Origins

This weapon was crafted during the third age by Bartholomew the Strong, the Kingdom of Ali's finest blacksmith. It took almost 6 months to gather the materials for this fine weapon and another 6 months just to finish its construction. This weapon was meant to be ten times stronger than a cavalryman's longsword and five times as light. The highest priest of Ali also had a stake in the game, purifying this sword on the top of Mount Sudak, making this weapon super effective against demonic foes.

Powers & Abilities

This sword is not only the fastest and strongest in all the kingdom of Ali. It also is the only weapon that can deal damage to demonic or undead foes. This capability makes this item class II since it bestows upon its wielder abilities beyond the metaphysical.

Limitations

This sword has few limitations other than its enormous length, which may make it difficult for shorter users to wield.

As a side note, it also (like almost all nonmagical swords) has a limited range.
