Tundra Force

Stats:

Ability Immense Power
Height 5' 7''
Width 1 foot
Length 3.5 inches
Weight 10,000 pounds
Item Class VII

Origins:

Inventor The Lords of Ice
Aliases Killer Frost
Purpose Defensive

Origins

The Lords of Ice, having heard of the Destiny Stalker's construction, decided to build a weapon of their own, and so, the Tundra Force was crafted. Its creators had a mindset of that of a defensive one and thus gave the sword immense power but also the ability to provide great protection. The sword can generate seemingly impenetrable thick layers of ice that can accomplish tasks like shielding a planet from the likes of crashing meteors.

The sword was made from a metal that solidifies only at absolute zero. The Gems of Power in the sword's cross guard give it its seemingly unimaginable strength, and they also keep the blade from melting.

Powers & Abilities

Tundra Force gives its bearer the ability to control all things water & ice. It is said to be the only weapon (along with the Dragon Slayer) that can rival the Destiny Stalker's power.

Limitations

As with all swords of power, the limitation of the item is not inherent in the sword itself. For the flaws of the mortal and those of the limitful, creep into the blade's grand design.
