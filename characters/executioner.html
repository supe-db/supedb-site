BackStory

The Executioner appeared much later in Vigilante-X's career, after the do-gooder had gained much-needed experience and fighting prowess. However, Vigilante-X's ego also had grown with victory, becoming inflated and unprepared for perhaps his most fierce opponent.

The Executioner does not seek to outsmart, outgun, or overpower Vigilante-X; instead, he seeks to destroy him, or at least the idea of him, the thought in any comic nerd's head that they could become a superhero. This is because the Executioner believes to truly break a man, you can't merely vanquish him; no, you must destroy everything he stood for.

Affiliations

At this time, the Executioner has no affiliations, although he seems to have an excessive hatred for Vigilante-X & the group known as H.E.R.O.

Powers & Abilities

The Executioner is a peak human in terms of strength, speed, and durability. He also gets a defensive boost from the bulletproof, knifeproof, or blunt-force-proof clothing he wears. In other words, his suit's protective layer varies based on what opponent or opponents he is up against. His energy stat is mainly derived from his gun and sword use; however, his military-grade percussion grenades give him a slight edge above the typical pistol-carrying criminal. Finally, the Executioner gets a psionics boost for his use of ninjutsu and other illusory practices, such as when he utilizes black smoke pellets or other distraction mechanisms.
