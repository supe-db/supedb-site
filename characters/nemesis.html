Affiliations

Nemesis is the leader of the Syndicate, a ragtag team of supervillains desperate for complete and total control (and a tad bit of revenge).

Powers & Abilities

Nemesis' Centurion roots give him superspeed, superstrength, and superdurability. The tech enhancements he made to his body aid these stats but also give him the ability to fly and the power to draw his opponents closer to him (this capability is due to his manipulations of their gravitational fields).

His titanium-adamantine alloy suit is seemingly impenetrable, and the force field he generates around his body does not help things. His suit is also capable of firing high, concentrated blasts of gamma energy through his palm. As for the yellow markers on his stats profile, they all come from a set of some of his most highly advanced upgrades.
