BackStory

Not much is known about Omni other than his desire to travel to remote planets and conquer them, claiming them for his galactic emperor. But it doesn't stop there; Omni not only invades those planets in his universe, but he also travels to other universes in order to fuel his nonstop desire for control and devastation.

Affiliations

At this time, Omni has no known affiliations. Note, there are those who claim he was once a citizen of New Mascarro; however, no concrete evidence has come in to back this up.

Powers & Abilities

Omni can manipulate the gravitational fields around him in order to fly. His skin is made up of some unknown but extremely durable metal, a polymer concoction, and the remains of a supercompressed dying planet. Omni is able to project large beams of violent energy that can seemingly tear planets apart, ripping right through them but not outright exploding them.

Omni has a keen intellect, most notably from his quantum computer mind, able to solve calculations that his mortal counterparts cannot even begin to understand. Despite his great intellect, Omni doesn't always seem in control, often distracted and conflicted by an even darker, more twisted alternate persona than his own.

Omni is also capable of demonstrating great feats of strength and speed, preferring to speed blitz his opponents as opposed to wasting time monologuing.
