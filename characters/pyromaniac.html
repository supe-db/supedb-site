BackStory

Give it up for the Pyro Guy, a crowd-pleaser, every kid's favorite superhero, and each single mother's biggest crush. He is a rad, flame-wielding, supercharged hothead with one hell of an afterburn. Pyro Guy's suit packs quite the punch, emitting flames more furious and hotter than the surface of the sun.

However, when he is not performing for kids, Pyro Guy has an alternate persona otherwise known as the Pyromaniac. A no-nonsense do-gooder who keeps villainous machines at bay from the inhabitants of his city.

Affiliations

Pyro Guy has no known affiliations at this time.

Powers & Abilities

Pyro Guy's suit is the source of his strength, speed, and durability, padded to not only take a hit but also the heat from intense flames hotter than the sun. Pyro Guy has a fuel pack strapped to his back; tubing from the pack runs into the palms of his hands. Little triggers on his fingers control when and if Pyro Guy decides to emit a burst of flames. Pyro Guy also has experimented with some metal attraction tech and associated traps, explaining why he has a psionics stat of 1.
