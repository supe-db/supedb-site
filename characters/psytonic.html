BackStory

Within this center is a miraculous prize fit for no mere man or ordinary mortal but a king's stone. For what the stone grants for their user is one wish, one seemingly boundless eternal wish. Affiliations

Psytonic is affiliated with the Land of Game, a cursed region filled with dungeons, dragons, demons, monsters, valleys, labyrinths, and a ton more other mystical beings than any young adventurer could hope for. The rules are simple: the Land only appears to those that are worthy or to those that are chosen.

Also, if a hero were to fail on his journey to capture the stone in the center of the labyrinth, then all the creatures he had slain in the prior levels are revived, seemingly reset and reamped up for their next challenger. The dark magic that binds this place is responsible for the force that gives the Land of Game's creatures mortality.

Powers & Abilities

Psytonic is a fire-breathing, palladium-armor-wearing demon who is equipped with long metal blades on the tips of his hands. His hide is thick, and his armor is thicker, considering it is made of one of the toughest materials in all the land. His blades are sharp and can cut through flesh, steel, or even diamond with relative ease. Even if those last feats don't scare their foe, Psytonic's near-atomic breath of pure plasma sure will. Overall, his immense strength, blinding speed, and pyrotechnics sure make him a daunting foe, especially since he only fights on his home turf, a twisting, turning, & shifting labyrinth.
