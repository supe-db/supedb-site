BackStory

The team is led by Queen Bee (center); second in command is Night Howl (center left); the muscle is Man Tiger (center right); the spy is Barracuda (far left); and finally, last but not least, the most cunning is Komodo (far right). These five members make up the royal family of the planet Animaliax; however, there are hundreds of millions more inhabitants.

These inhabitants also resemble the cross between a human and several other different animals. Animaliaxian (aka Humanoidian) society is somewhat equivalent to that of Homo sapiens around the early 10th to 15th century, in other words, the end of the Middle Ages. Their technological advances, although similar, are not exactly equivalent, considering Animaliaxian adaptation needs and resources available to them are different than those of humans.

Affiliations

The humanoids are affiliated with no outside groups. Their planet's population size is estimated to be between 500,000,000 and 650,000,000 inhabitants. Their society is a monarchical one, led by the five royals and their corresponding subjects. At the top is Queen Bee, the Queen of Animaliax. Second in command is Night Howl, a humanoid wolf-like creature.

Powers & Abilities

All humanoids essentially have the intelligence of a human (although it varies amongst members) with the strength, speed, and durability of an animal. Their weaponry at this time is somewhat primitive, mainly consisting of catapults, crossbows, and primitive bombs as opposed to guns, fighter jets, or other superior forms of artillery. As a side note, the main reason for their lack of advanced munitions is that their superior athletic capabilities make any form of additional weaponry mostly unnecessary.
